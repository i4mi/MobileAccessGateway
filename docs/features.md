The Mobile Access Gateway provides translation from REST to SOAP variants of IHE transaction:

- ITI-65 to ITI-41
- ITI-66 to ITI-18
- ITI-67 to ITI-18 or ITI-57
- ITI-68 to ITI-43
- PHARM-5 to PHARM-1
- ITI-78 to ITI-47
- ITI-83 to ITI-45
- ITI-93 to ITI-44
- ITI-104 to ITI-44 or ITI-47
- ITI-71 to ITI-40
- RESTful Get X-User Assertion to Get X-User Assertion
